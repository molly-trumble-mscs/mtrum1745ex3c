Music Style Options

Classic music

Choose from a variety of instrument combinations and pieves ranging from classical standards to graceful arrangments of more modern compositions. Rest easy with delightfully simple guitar and violin, raise your spirits with a peppy string trio, or experience the calming effect of a harp and flute duet. Perfect for a wedding!

Instrument Options

  • String quartet (2 Violins, Viola, Cello)
  • Violin, Viola & Cello
  • 2 Violins & Viola
  • Violin duo
  • Violin & Viola
  • Violin or Viola & Cello
  • Flute, Violin or Viola & Harp

Bluegrass music

This rousing style is typically played by instruments such as fiddle, banjo, mandolin, double bass and is more popular for autumn outdoor events. From original compositions to good old fashioned gospel tunes, bluegrass music is sure to keep your audience engaged. If you looking for somthing upbeat, this is the way to go for a toe tapping good time!

Instrument Options

  • Fiddle, Mandolin, banjo, Guitar & Double Bass
  • Mandolin, banjo & Double Bass
  • Fiddle & Guitar
  • Fiddle, Guitar & Double Bass
  • Fiddle, Banjo & Guitar
  • Mandolin, Fiddle & Guitar

Irish & Folk music

It's back to the old and ancient days with this style. Irish whistle, fiddle or flute accompanied by guitar or folk harp or even accordion creates an easy-going nostalgic atmosphere that will bring back memories. This style is frequently requested for things like birthdays, aniversaries, festivals and fundraisors.

Instrument Options

  • Fiddle, Whistle, Harp, Accordian & Guitar
  • Whistle & Harp or Guitar
  • Fiddle, Accordian & Guitar
  • Fiddle, Whistle & Guitar
  • Whistle & Accordian
  • Fiddle & Guitar
